Research Topic: E-Commerce Popularity & Consumer Demographics Survey

1. Introduction & Methodology

To analyze the popularity and demographic penetration of electronic commerce (e-commerce), a structured survey was conducted among 20 individual participants, including classmates, friends, family members, and coworkers. The survey measured shopping behaviors, purchasing frequencies, age, gender, education distribution, and platform preferences.
